Clarify — common questions

Is fasting required if I am sick?
If illness prevents safe fasting, a person may break the fast and make it up later; consult a scholar for individual cases.
Can I combine prayers while traveling?
Many schools permit combining certain prayers when traveling; check the details of your madhhab or follow a trusted contemporary ruling.
What is the etiquette for seeking knowledge?
Sincerity, verified sources, and humility are essential. Prefer primary texts and qualified teachers.
